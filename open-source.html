What's so great about

"open source"?

An open-source project doesn't just share a cool app, it also shares how the app was made.

You can download and play this game, and you can also head over to the hueman GitHub page to look at the source code.

It's difficult for an open-source project to make money:
if you try to use advertisements or in-app purchases, anyone can just copy the source code, get rid of the ads & paywalls, and re-release it for free.


But there's a whole lot that makes open-source great:

  • It's educational! If you see something cool and wonder "how did they do that?", just go to the source and see for yourself.
  • Get free help! There are a lot of games with wonderful modding communities, filled with passionate people who make the experience better for everyone, without even being paid. When you're 100% open source, it's that much easier for your creation to grow and develop in beautiful ways.
